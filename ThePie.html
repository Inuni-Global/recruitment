Within the GUS Ecosystem

Hosted at GUS Group institutions in London — a private education group operating 31 universities across four continents, with 122,000 students on campus and 20 million more online. Delegates are introduced to its academic leadership, its institutions, and the operating model behind one of the world's largest private higher-education networks.

The Programme

Two days convened with international leaders of higher education — heads of universities, ministries and academic institutions, contributing to the panels and roundtables as peers rather than as an audience. Sessions on TNE strategy, AI in admissions, accreditation and ethical partnership. A guided showcase of the GUS Group's London universities. Closing with the Summit Declaration.
